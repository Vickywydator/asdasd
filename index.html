The Vision

The Art of Living Well

Founded by Vicky Guttmann Wydator, our firm operates on the belief that styling is the heartbeat of any interior. While architecture builds the shell, styling provides the soul. We are a boutique agency located in S達o Paulo, dedicated to transforming sterile spaces into inviting sanctuaries.

Unlike traditional renovations that focus on demolition, our expertise lies in the transformative power of furniture curation, art placement, textile layering, and lighting. We work with what exists to elevate it to its highest potential, ensuring that every corner tells a story.

Our approach is deeply personal and collaborative. We listen to the rhythm of your daily life and the aspirations of your brand to create environments that are not only visually stunning but emotionally resonant. Perfection is found in the details.

Whether you are refreshing a long-time home or launching a new commercial space, VICKYGW brings a refined eye and a network of Brazil's finest artisans to your project.

Methodology

From Concept to Composition

1

2

Once the concept is approved, we scour the market. We coordinate orders, track shipments, and manage quality control for furniture, textiles, and art, handling all vendor interactions.

3

The final phase is the most exciting. Our team arrives on-site to install furniture, hang art, and style accessories. We obsess over the placement of every object to ensure perfect balance.
